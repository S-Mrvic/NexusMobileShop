About us

Discover a World of Smartphones at Nexus Shop

Welcome to Nexus Shop, your premier online destination for suprime costumer experience. We are dedicated to bringing you the latest and greatest mobile devices and services, ensuring that you stay connected and ahead in today's fast-paced digital landscape.

Nexus Shop was born in 2000 in United Kingdom when Paul Richard created the first independent distribution chain of mobile telephony: Carphone Warehouse. Today Nowadays Nexus Shop has a presence in Europe (United Kingdom, Spain, Ireland, Holland, Germany, Switzerland and Serbia) and we are working towards greater expansion.

The company is currently managed by more than 50 professionals, enhancing consumer experience and great availability for our customers in the always revolutionary world of mobile technology. Today more than ever before, we help our clients to find the perfect product for them and we make sure you always get the maximum match.

Nexus Shop is not just an online store; it's a vibrant community of smartphone enthusiasts. Connect with like-minded individuals through our blog, social media platforms, and interactive forums. Our mission is to be at customer disposal at any time, increase customer awareness and make our clients always happy!
